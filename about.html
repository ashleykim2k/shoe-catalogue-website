Shoes.

MIA is a luxury footwear catalogue designed by Michelle, Isabella, and Ashley. This website serves as an outlet for shoe lovers to explore various styles and silhouettes from top designers within the luxury fashion realm. Each season, the MIA team carefully selects the styles to be featured on the website. MIA keeps up with the latest trends in the ever-evolving fashion community. MIA serves as a platform for individuals to draw inspiration from while developing a passion for footwear design and innovation.
